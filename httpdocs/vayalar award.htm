PK Balakrishnan was conferred with the Vayalar award during 1978, the second year after institution of the same in 1997.

Vayalar Award is given for the best literary work in Malayalam. The award was instituted in the year 1977, by the Vayalar Ramavarma Memorial Trust in memory of renowned poet and lyricist Vayalar Ramavarma(1928-1975).
